Free Digital Scout

Find out if we can provide service to your property with a free digital scout. The way our wireless internet technology works, you need a clear line of sight between our tower and your house in order to get internet service. We can check the line of sight for you using our topography map and LiDAR technology.

HOW IT WORKS:

  • Request your scout using the form below.
  • Our technicians will check your address and find out if you are serviceable.
  • You will be informed of the results.
Please Note: The digital scout is free and we will not ask for a payment method to perform the scout. If you are a good candidate for an install, we will invite you to sign up for service. Please be aware that certain factors, such as new construction or tree growth, can mean that service is occasionally not available to addresses when we thought it would be. If you choose to sign up for service you will not be charged an install fee unless your install is successful.
