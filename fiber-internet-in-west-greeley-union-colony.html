Lightgig Communications’ High-Speed Fiber Internet comes to West Greeley - Union Colony

The Union Colony Neighborhood, an exciting new subdivision in West Greeley, Colorado, proudly announces the arrival of Lightgig Communications’ state-of-the-art fiber optic internet service. Developed by Journey Homes, Union Colony is a community designed for modern living, featuring parks, amenities, and easy access to Greeley’s vibrant local culture. Located near the major cross streets of Business 34 and 83rd Ave, this neighborhood now benefits from Lightgig’s ultra-fast internet, enhancing connectivity for residents and supporting the community’s growth.

Lightgig Communications, a Northern Colorado-based provider, brings its fiber-to-the-home technology to Union Colony, offering symmetrical gigabit speeds of up to 10,000 Mbps. This service ensures residents can seamlessly work from home, stream entertainment, and utilize smart home devices without interruption, meeting the demands of today’s digital lifestyle.

Lightgig Communications’ Service Highlights

  1. Blazing-Fast Speeds: Symmetrical upload and download speeds of up to 10,000 Mbps, 40 times faster than the average 25 Mbps connection, perfect for gaming, streaming, and remote work.
  2. Reliable Fiber-to-the-Home: Fiber optic cables run directly to each home, providing consistent performance with minimal latency, unaffected by weather conditions like wind (99.99% network uptime).
  3. Local Support and Flexible Plans: Month-to-month plans with no long-term contracts, free installation offers, and locally-based customer service accessible via phone or email for a personalized experience.
