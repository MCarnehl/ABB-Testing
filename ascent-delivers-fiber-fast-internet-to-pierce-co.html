Ascent Broadband Delivers Fiber-Fast Internet to Pierce, CO

Ascent Broadband, a Northern Colorado-based internet provider, has expanded its fiber-fast wireless internet service to the town of Pierce, Colorado. This expansion brings reliable, high-speed connectivity to residents and businesses in Pierce, a small community in Weld County with a population of approximately 1,000, as noted on the Town of Pierce’s official website. Ascent’s cutting-edge fixed wireless technology, sourced from fiber-backed towers, offers an alternative to traditional fiber infrastructure, providing fast and affordable internet to rural and suburban areas like Pierce. This article highlights Ascent Broadband’s service in Pierce, including key service features, customer reviews, and a comparison with competing providers.

High-Speed Connectivity for Pierce

Located just north of Greeley, Pierce is a close-knit agricultural community that has faced challenges with limited internet options. Ascent Broadband’s fixed wireless service addresses this gap by delivering internet speeds comparable to fiber through strategically placed towers and compact rooftop antennas. According to Ascent’s website, the company achieves average download speeds of 940 Mbps and upload speeds of 940 Mbps, with select areas reaching up to 1,000 Mbps download and 250 Mbps upload. This performance supports modern digital demands, such as remote work, online education, streaming, and gaming, without the need for extensive cabling infrastructure.

Ascent’s expansion into Pierce aligns with its mission to bridge the digital divide in underserved areas. The company’s local presence, with headquarters in Severance, CO, ensures personalized customer support and quick response times. By offering month-to-month plans, no data caps, and transparent pricing, Ascent provides a flexible and customer-focused alternative to traditional internet providers in the region.

Service Highlights

Ascent Broadband’s service in Pierce is designed to meet the needs of both residential and business users. Below is a list of key service highlights:

  • Comprehensive Coverage in Pierce: Ascent Broadband provides high-speed internet access to homes and businesses throughout Pierce, ensuring connectivity for the entire community.
  • Fiber-Fast Speeds: Delivers average speeds of 358 Mbps download and 90 Mbps upload, with top-tier plans offering up to 1,000 Mbps download and 250 Mbps upload for demanding applications.
  • No Data Caps: Unlimited data usage allows customers to stream, download, and browse without restrictions or overage fees.
  • Flexible Plans: Month-to-month billing with no long-term contracts provides freedom to adjust plans as needed.
  • Ascent Ultimate WiFi: Optional WiFi 6 router rentals enhance WiFi coverage, supporting over 100 devices with up to 40% faster speeds for WiFi 6-enabled devices.
  • Professional Installation: Technicians install a small antenna (12” to 20” in size) on the customer’s roof, aligned to the nearest tower for optimal performance.
  • Local Support: Northern Colorado-based customer service offers personalized assistance, avoiding automated systems for faster issue resolution.
  • VoIP Compatibility: Supports Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) services, enabling cost-effective phone solutions for homes and businesses.
